Good Idea / Bad Idea

Business Plan

Executive Summary

Good Idea/Bad Idea is a social decision-making platform that allows users to vote on ideas through an intuitive swipe interface. The app combines the addictive mechanics of dating apps with practical decision-making use cases, creating a unique market position.

Universal Appeal

Works for personal, social, and business decisions

Familiar UX

Leverages proven swipe mechanics users already understand

Social Integration

Enables group decision-making and idea sharing

Gamification

Points, badges, and leaderboards drive engagement

Market Analysis

Target Market Segments

Primary Market (18-35 years old)

  • Size: ~2.5 billion people globally
  • Characteristics: Tech-savvy, social media active, decision-making needs
  • Use Cases: Personal choices, social planning, entertainment decisions

Secondary Market (35-50 years old)

  • Size: ~1.8 billion people globally
  • Characteristics: Professional decision-makers, group leaders
  • Use Cases: Team decisions, project planning, family choices

Tertiary Market (Business/Enterprise)

  • Size: Growing market for decision-making tools
  • Characteristics: Teams, organizations, event planners
  • Use Cases: Team building, product feedback, event planning

Market Size & Opportunity

Total Addressable Market (TAM)

  • Social Media Users: 4.9 billion globally
  • Decision-Making Apps: $2.3 billion market (2023)
  • Mobile App Market: $935 billion globally

Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

  • Active Social Media Users: 3.2 billion
  • Decision-Making Tool Users: 500 million
  • Estimated SAM: $15-25 billion

Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)

  • Year 1 Target: 100,000 users
  • Year 3 Target: 1 million users
  • Year 5 Target: 5 million users

Competitive Analysis

Direct Competitors

Decision Roulette

  • Strengths: Simple, free
  • Weaknesses: No social features, limited use cases
  • Market Position: Basic utility app

Coin Flip

  • Strengths: Extremely simple
  • Weaknesses: Too basic, no social aspect
  • Market Position: Basic utility

Decision Tree

  • Strengths: Comprehensive, analytical
  • Weaknesses: Complex, not mobile-friendly
  • Market Position: Professional tool

Competitive Advantages

  • Unique Positioning: Only app combining swipe mechanics with decision-making
  • Social Features: Built-in group functionality
  • Versatility: Works for personal, social, and business decisions
  • Mobile-First: Optimized for mobile decision-making

Business Model

Revenue Streams

1. Freemium Model

  • Free Tier: Basic features, limited ideas per day
  • Premium Tier ($4.99/month): Unlimited ideas, advanced analytics
  • Pro Tier ($9.99/month): Team features, custom branding, API access

2. Advertising

  • Display Ads: Non-intrusive banner ads
  • Sponsored Ideas: Promoted content from brands
  • Category Sponsorship: Branded categories

3. Data Insights

  • Market Research: Aggregate decision patterns
  • Trend Analysis: Popular ideas by demographic
  • Business Intelligence: Decision-making insights

4. White-Label Solutions

  • Enterprise Version: Custom branded for companies
  • Event Planning: Specialized version for organizers
  • Educational: Version for schools and universities

Marketing Strategy

User Acquisition

Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-12)

  • Target: 100,000 users
  • Channels: Social media marketing, influencer partnerships, app store optimization, viral sharing

Phase 3: Scale (Months 13-24)

  • Target: 1 million users
  • Channels: Paid advertising, PR and media coverage, strategic partnerships, international expansion
